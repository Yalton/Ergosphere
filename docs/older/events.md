# Events & Supernatural System

## Event Types & Escalation

### Days 1-2: Subtle Events
- **Entity appears and follows player around** - Brief appearances, mostly peripheral vision
- **Player is teleported to random room** - Rare, momentary disorientation
- **Blood decals appear on walls and floors for a moment and then disappear** - Environmental storytelling
- **Spooky sound plays at one predetermined position** - Atmospheric audio cues

### Days 3-4: Active Events  
- **Smaller black hole appears in hallway** - Physics anomaly, blocks certain paths temporarily
- **Fire appears and must be put out by player** - Interactive emergency event
- **Shadowy figure appears at end of hall** - More persistent visual disturbances
- **Black hole accretion disk color changes** - Visual indicator of increasing instability
- **Player is teleported to Maze and then warped back to the station** - Longer displacement events
- **Player vision goes wonky** - Screen distortion effects
- **Power Outage** - Systems failure requiring manual intervention
- **Oxygen Tank Filter Failure** - Life support emergency
- **Generator Heatsink needs replacing** - Critical system maintenance
- **Flesh appears on the walls** - Reality distortion intensifies
- **Lightning and smoke wall appears blocking player's path** - Environmental obstacles

### Day 5: Overwhelming Events
- **Environment outside gets darker** - Visual indication of final phase
- **Eyes appear all around the station** - Entity's presence becomes undeniable
- **Mysterious Obelisk appears** - Key story/ending element manifests

## Emergency Tasks & Consequences

### Emergency Task Types
1. **Oxygen Tank Filter Failure**
   - Warning: "Oxygen generator filter failure. Breathable atmosphere degrading. Manual intervention required."
   - **Consequence if failed**: Player health slowly decreases, vision becomes blurry
   - **Location**: Engine room or maintenance bay
   - **Solution**: Replace filter component

2. **Power Outage**  
   - Warning: "Master fuse tripped. Manual reset required in engine room."
   - **Consequence if failed**: Lights go out, some systems offline, harder to navigate
   - **Location**: Engine room electrical panel
   - **Solution**: Flip master breaker switch

3. **Generator Heatsink Failure**
   - Warning: "Engine heat-sink critical failure. Core temperature rising. Replace immediately."  
   - **Consequence if failed**: Risk of reactor shutdown, station systems failing
   - **Location**: Engine room
   - **Solution**: Use wrench (ordered Day 2) to replace heatsink component

4. **Fire Emergency**
   - Warning: "Fire detected. Suppression systems offline. Manual intervention required."
   - **Consequence if failed**: Fire spreads, damages station systems, creates smoke hazards
   - **Location**: Various (random spawn)
   - **Solution**: Use fire extinguisher

5. **Radiation Exposure**
   - Warning: "Sejanus emitting anomalous Hawking radiation. Close windows immediately."
   - **Consequence if failed**: Player takes radiation damage, hallucinations increase
   - **Location**: Observation deck
   - **Solution**: Close protective shutters on windows

### Consequence Escalation
- **Day 1-2**: Minor inconveniences (flickering lights, slow systems)
- **Day 3-4**: Moderate impacts (health effects, system failures)  
- **Day 5**: Severe consequences (life-threatening failures, reality breakdown)

## Environmental Storytelling Events

### Subtle Changes
- Personal items from previous crews appearing in strange places
- Scratch marks inside maintenance shafts  
- Areas with too many emergency supplies
- Rooms with furniture arranged to block something
- Burn marks in places that shouldn't have seen fire
- Warning signs for hazards that don't exist (or do they?)

### Reality Distortions
- Station layout subtly changes between day cycles
- Clocks showing different times in different parts of station
- Maps or diagrams that don't match actual layout
- Doors that lead to different rooms than expected
- Corridors that seem longer or shorter than they should be

### Entity Manifestations
- Shadows moving independently of their sources
- Reflections in surfaces showing different scenes
- Text on screens briefly changing to unknown symbols
- Equipment activating by itself
- Sounds of crew members in places they couldn't be

## Event Trigger System

### Time-Based Triggers
- **Fixed schedule**: Certain events always happen on specific days
- **Random windows**: Events can occur within specific time ranges
- **Progressive thresholds**: Events become more frequent as days progress

### Action-Based Triggers  
- **Observation triggers**: Looking at black hole for too long
- **Location triggers**: Entering specific areas at certain times
- **Task triggers**: Completing or failing certain tasks
- **Interaction triggers**: Touching specific objects or areas

### State-Based Triggers
- **Sanity thresholds**: Events increase as player's psychological state deteriorates  
- **System status**: Equipment failures trigger supernatural responses
- **Day progression**: Each day has its own event pool and intensities

## Audio Design for Events

### Supernatural Audio Cues
- **Low-frequency rumbles** - Entity presence
- **Whispers in static** - Hidden in radio communications
- **Mechanical sounds becoming organic** - Technology infected by entity
- **Heartbeat synchronization** - Player proximity to entity influence
- **Impossible acoustics** - Sounds from directions that shouldn't exist

### Progressive Audio Distortion
- **Day 1-2**: Subtle audio glitches, occasional static
- **Day 3-4**: Clear distortions, voices in noise, reverse audio
- **Day 5**: Reality breakdown, impossible sounds, entity "voice"

### Environmental Audio Events
- Sounds of previous crew members
- Equipment running in areas that should be empty
- Footsteps following the player
- Conversations in languages that don't exist
- The "sound" of the void - what silence sounds like when it's alive

## Entity Behavior Patterns

### Observation Phase (Days 1-2)
- Watches from periphery
- Minimal direct interaction
- Tests player awareness
- Subtle reality alterations

### Engagement Phase (Days 3-4)  
- Direct manifestations
- Reality manipulation
- System interference
- Psychological pressure

### Consumption Phase (Day 5)
- Full presence
- Reality breakdown
- Direct communication attempts
- Final ritual completion

The entity becomes progressively bolder and more desperate as the ritual nears completion, shifting from subtle observer to active participant in the final awakening sequence.
