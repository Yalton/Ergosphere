You are the current steward of Thoth Observatory, a deep space research station where humanity's future hangs in the balance. Aboard this isolated facility you must maintain critical systems, gather observational data, and manage increasingly unstable equipment—all while the station's machine intelligence withholds information it deems unnecessary for you to know. As days pass and systems fail with mounting frequency, you must balance routine tasks like calibrating equipment and operating station systems against emergency repairs that could mean the difference between completing your mission and catastrophic failure. The miracle of the Sejanus Singularity may be mankind's only path to FTL communication, but first you must survive your shift as stweard in the cold depths of space, where every decision weighs heavy and the margin for error grows thinner with each passing day.
