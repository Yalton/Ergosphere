**Why Events First:**
1. **You already have momentum** - You've got 11 events working, you understand the system, and you can knock out more quickly
2. **Player retention** - A monster without a rich, spooky environment is just a chase sequence. Events create the atmosphere that makes the monster terrifying when it finally appears
3. **Easier to test and iterate** - Events are isolated, you can test them individually. Monster AI touches everything and will be a debugging nightmare if your event system isn't solid
4. **Foundation for the monster** - Many of your unimplemented events (Reality Stutter, The Maze, Dream Sequence) could tie into how the monster manipulates reality. Get these working first so the monster can leverage them
